## 2024-04-03 - Improved Medications Empty State
**Learning:** Found that the 'No medications logged' empty state was just plain text, making it look broken or unstyled compared to the rest of the polished UI.
**Action:** When encountering empty states for tracking or logging sections, always replace plain text with a structured layout containing a relevant icon (e.g., `lucide-react`), a clear heading, and a supportive subtitle to guide the user and maintain visual consistency.
