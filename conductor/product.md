# Product Guide: Vireo Wellness

## Vision
Vireo Wellness is a progressive web application designed to empower individuals in managing their daily wellness. By providing a centralized, intelligently designed single-page application (SPA), Vireo simplifies the tracking of critical health metrics like blood glucose, nutrition, and daily habits. The application aims to replace fragmented tools with an aesthetically pleasing, seamless, and mobile-optimized experience that feels native.

## Target Audience
Individuals looking to take control of their daily health metrics, particularly those tracking blood sugar trends, monitoring dietary intake, and managing smart health devices.

## Core Features
- **Dashboard Insights:** At-a-glance summaries of health metrics, including nutritional stats, interactive blood glucose charts, and activity tracking.
- **Comprehensive Meal Logging:** Categorized meal logging from a built-in food database with macro-nutritional breakdowns (calories, carbs, fat, protein).
- **Health Device Management:** Interface to manage smart health devices via Bluetooth.
- **Timeline & Date Picker:** Intuitive navigation to view historical logs and future wellness goals.

## Strategic Goals
- Provide a robust frontend architecture ready for eventual backend integration.
- Deliver an accessible, eye-strain-friendly interface via a permanent dark theme.
- Ensure high code quality standards through automated testing and strict linting.
