# Product Guidelines: Vireo Wellness

## Design Principles
- **Clarity & Focus:** As a health-centric application, data presentation must be clean, uncluttered, and easy to interpret at a glance.
- **Accessibility:** Ensure high contrast within the permanent dark theme design, adhering to WCAG standards where possible. Interactive elements should be large enough for comfortable mobile tapping.
- **Consistency:** Maintain a consistent visual language using Tailwind CSS utility classes. Re-use existing components (like stat cards and modals) to provide a predictable user experience.

## UX Principles
- **Frictionless Entry:** Adding a meal or logging data should require the minimum number of clicks possible.
- **Immediate Feedback:** Provide visual confirmations for actions (e.g., successful device pairing, saving a log) using fluid animations.
- **Graceful Degradation:** The application should handle missing or empty states gracefully, providing helpful nudges or default displays rather than blank screens.

## Tone and Voice
- **Empathetic & Encouraging:** Health tracking can be stressful; use language that is supportive and positive rather than clinical or judgmental.
- **Concise:** Keep labels, tooltips, and instructions short and to the point.
- **Professional but Approachable:** Communicate reliability without being overly formal.
